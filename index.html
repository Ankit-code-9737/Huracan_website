How does it work?

Create Task

Download the software file, create your billing profile, create the task to your desired item.

Purchasing the Item

Begin your task. The automation tool will take over by locating, monitoring, and then checking out your desired item.

Check Outs

Checkout confirmations will be sent to your desired designation via a Discord webhook, along with an order confirmation via the email provided. It’s as easy as that!
